# Weekly Training Plan

This section focuses on building a complete weekly training plan for preparing for a 5K race. A good plan should balance endurance, speed, longer runs, and recovery so the runner can improve without overtraining. Each page in this section explains one part of the weekly structure and shows how the pieces connect.
