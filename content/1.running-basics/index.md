# 🏃 Running Basics
# 🏃 5K Race Preparation Knowledge Base

Welcome to my **5K Race Preparation Knowledge Base**, a connected resource designed to explain the major parts of preparing for a successful 5K race.

A 5K is a distance of 3.1 miles, but preparing for one involves much more than simply running the race distance repeatedly. A complete training approach includes developing basic running skills, organizing weekly workouts, improving speed, building strength, recovering between training sessions, supporting the body with proper nutrition and hydration, and preparing an effective race-day strategy.

This knowledge base organizes those topics into seven connected sections. Each section contains detailed pages that explain individual concepts and connect to related resources throughout the site.

> Successful 5K preparation is not built from one workout. It comes from combining training, recovery, nutrition, and strategy into one consistent system.

## 🎯 Purpose of This Knowledge Base

The goal of this site is to provide a clear and organized introduction to 5K preparation.

It is designed for runners who may be:

- Preparing for their first 5K
- Returning to running after time away
- Trying to understand structured training
- Looking to improve a previous 5K performance
- Interested in learning how different parts of running preparation connect

No single training approach works perfectly for every runner. Experience, fitness, health, schedule, and individual goals can all influence how someone prepares.

This knowledge base focuses on explaining the major concepts so readers can better understand how different parts of 5K preparation work together.
